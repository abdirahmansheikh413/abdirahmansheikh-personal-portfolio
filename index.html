Where it starts

My story doesn't begin with code. It begins with resilience.

As a baby I survived a serious brain injury. At six, the seizures began — years of epilepsy that didn't ask permission, and a long road most people never saw. In 2015, surgery changed everything. I've been seizure-free ever since.

I don't share this to be admired. I share it because it's the reason I build the way I do: patiently, with discipline, and with deep respect for the people the world tends to overlook. I learn differently — and that's exactly why accessibility isn't a feature to me. It's personal.
